About Us

CoreLogs is a Business enabling website for SMEs and a platform to connect the SMEs to the world and vice versa. The CoreLogs Profile of an SMEs is now the front page of that SME on internet. Apart from that, CoreLogs provides the SMEs with a unique and sophisticated product listing platform. Here they can select their target audience and get their products featured in the Marketplace. CoreLogs also provides the SAE teams and Racing enthusiasts with a platform where they can buy and sell mechanical components and racing accessories.
